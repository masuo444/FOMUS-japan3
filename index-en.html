What is Masu?

Masu began as a measuring tool and evolved into an auspicious vessel for celebrations, woven into everyday life and milestones in Japan.

FOMUS reimagines the meaning of masu through technology and community, transforming it into a communication tool that connects culture and the world.

Culture Rooted in Masu

An object that evolved with time

Measuring Tool

Originally used to measure rice and sake, a foundation of fairness in Japan.

Auspicious & Celebratory

Symbolizing growth and prosperity, it brings good fortune to celebrations.

Sake Vessel

With the aroma of hinoki, it deepens conversation and connection.
